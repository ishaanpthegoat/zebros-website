Sponsor the Zebros.

FTC Team 30415 is a youth robotics team that designs, builds, and programs competition robots, and teaches younger kids about STEM in our community. Sponsorship helps us fund parts, registration, travel, and outreach.

A two-way partnership

Sponsorship with the Zebros is built to send value both ways. Here is what makes a strong fit.

🔁

Two-Way Referrals

We send our robotics families to your business, and your customers send students our way. Real customers in both directions.

📣

Local Visibility

Your name and logo in front of families at competitions, outreach events, and online all season long.

🏠

Shared Space

We can host our larger team events at your location, bringing new faces and foot traffic through your doors.
